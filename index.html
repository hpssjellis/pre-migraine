My Personal Migraine Prevention Site

Started March 26th, 2021. Absolutely no base in any Science, just my own thoughts after 27 years of migraine pain (Strangely correlated with the number of years I have been a parent). I have however had regular sugar induced headaches all my life. The difference is that regular headaches get some relief using Tylenol. For me Migraines have a distinct prodome an visual aura 20 minutes before pain and for which Tylenol has no effect.

My Migraine Background I used to make computer programs to monitor which foods correlated with my Migraines, but for the last 10 years I have eaten a meat and vegetables (typically high in Potassium) low salt diet that has done fine at migraine prevention as long as I stick to it. There's the rub, since sticking to it means having very little of the food listed below in the Rocksetta Migraine Prevention Testing

I do have a Chemistry Degree and worked as a trace substance Analytical Chemist, but what does that mean.
I also simplify Machine Learning and other complex Robotics and coding tasks for students and have taught Science and Physics for many years.

The Rocksetta 3 McDouble, Salted Avocado Migraine Prevention

To be fair it should be called the "5 Gram Creatine, Salted Avocado Migraine Prevention", since no one should ever have 3 McDoubles a day unless they are running a full marathon or swimming/biking for a few hours, but that is what you might have to eat to absorb 2-5 grams of Creatine, reference here
A 2020 reference for Creatine and migraine is here

Basic Concept:
  1. Morning have salted high Potassium low sugar foods like Avocado's, Sweat Potatoes, Mushrooms and Eggs.
  2. During the day eat normally and hydrate with occasional small amounts of salt
  3. Normal dinner but have more red meat/salmon and salt.
  4. Have a bit of Creatine Monohydrate some time during the day. (Body builders use if to store energy for the brain and muscles and help retain water in the cells)


So far this method seems to be doing well with the tests above. I will not know for sure if this method works fully until I start teaching school in September. Last year on startup when I was teaching some students in person and most of the class online I had 5 migraines in 10 days!
